01 what i do

Running and reporting on search campaigns

I build search campaigns, then I read the data they produce.

Structure, keywords, and ad copy on one side. Clickthrough, conversions, and quality scores on the other. The work is keeping those two in conversation.

A current focus is Google Ad Grants, the free search-advertising program Google offers eligible nonprofits. I manage one nonprofit's Ad Grants account on a volunteer basis. The program is strict: a 5% clickthrough-rate floor, quality-score minimums, conversion-based bidding. Staying inside those rules means watching the numbers closely.

For that I read straight from the Google Ads API, using Google's own official, read-only tooling to pull the metrics that show whether an account is compliant and performing. It reads performance data. It doesn't change campaigns.

Google Ads search

Ad Grants compliance

Turning raw account data (impressions, CTR, conversions, search terms) into a clear read on what's working.
